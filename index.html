The Sea Chest

The Sea Chest is all about an old box of a captain named Flint. In this chapter, Jim is the narrator who narrates the story of his life. After the death of his father and Billy (owner of the lodge where they stay), fear starts creeping into Jim and his mother’s mind. Jim who was an innocent boy turned into the phase of maturity and defends the problems coming to him and his mother. Death of captain caused a big trouble to Jim and his mother. As they needed the money from captain’s treasure box. They took the key from the dead body of captain and opened an old box named as “The Sea Chest” and the chapter is all about this old box and the treasures.

Jim is the main character of this story. Jim and his mother opens the old box of an unknown person and takes all the money, which belonged to them. After the blind beggar (one of the treasure hunter) approaches, fear starts creeping into Jim’s mother and she faints while running. The fear of losing his mother gives him a big terror and hides with his mother under a bridge. After losing his father and Billy, Jim fears to lose the ones who are interacted with him.

When a person starts fearing, there always comes a way to avoid it or to face it. Jim here in this chapter, face the fear and gets over with a young mind turning into maturity. Fear is the only emotion, which makes an individual strong enough to overcome it and change the difficulties of the real world. Fear is the emotion that creates positive impact on the person. An insecure person always wants someone whom they trust to overcome such fear.

Master od War-Bob Dylan

Master of War is a pacifistic song against war written by Bob Dylan in 1963. It is a Folk song released on the album “The Freewheelin’Bob Dylan”. Master of wars does not mean a person who is the leader of an army or arm machine, Master refers to the political leaders or the person who is leading the country.

People are forced to join the war not because the want but because the political leaders wants them to join the war. Dylan say that the war machine are not made by a manufacture company or by any people, but it is built by the so called government who live in the central house of the state. Government never go to the war but they are the one who create war. They are the one responsible for the war. There is never a war between country and people of country, the war is feed into the brain of citizen by the government.

The world is a free place for every human being living in here, but the dispute between to government creates war and the one who suffers from it is the local people not the government or so called leaders. And yes it is not an antiwar song, it does not say people to stop war but what Dylan is trying to say is that the war is created by the government for their own good not for the country or the people of the country.
